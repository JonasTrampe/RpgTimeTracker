Time Jumps
==========

A free-form jump text field (``hh:mm:ss`` or ``t.hh:mm:ss`` for days,
``-`` for backward) plus quick-select buttons (+1 hr, +8 hr, +1 day,
-1 hr, -1 day). The most recent jump (including via quick select or
bookmarks) can be undone with a button - an extended undo history keeps
more than just the single most recent jump.
