# RPG Time Tracker — Player Display

Shows the timeline sent by the GM, as well as images, videos, and sounds, on
a player's device on the local network.

- **Timeline**: current game time and running timers/alarms/intervals,
  always in sync with the GM.
- **Media**: images/videos automatically open in their own window,
  including slideshow navigation via arrow keys or click.
- **Connection**: connects to the GM control via LAN/mDNS discovery or a
  manual host address.

Runs entirely on your own local network — no data is sent to external
servers.
