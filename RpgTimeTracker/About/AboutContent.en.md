# RPG Time Tracker — GM Control

Controls game time, timers, alarms, and media for the session and sends them
live to connected player clients on the local network.

- **Chronometer**: fast-forward/rewind game time, timers/alarms/intervals with
  their own sound and optional trigger media.
- **Library**: prepare images, videos, and sounds and send them to all
  players with one click, including a slideshow gallery and volume/trim
  settings.
- **Network**: built-in server with LAN/mDNS discovery, no manual
  configuration needed on the player side.

Runs entirely on your own local network — no data is sent to external
servers.
